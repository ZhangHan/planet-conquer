About Us

PythonVSRuby (Or RubyVSPython) is an organization who concentrate on better technical commnucation between Pythoners and Rubyists.

All of the members in RubyVSPython organization are volunteers. We come here to contribute our time because we love Ruby or Python, or both of them.

Every year we will organize 3 events.
On April - New Platform "Celebrating" Contest
On August - Hackathon to make a sprint of the platform.
On December - Final Contest to decide the name will be "RubyVSPython" or "PythonVSRuby" for next year.

Our Vision

  • We use programming to communicate.
  • Friendship first, competition second.
  • Dare to innovate.
